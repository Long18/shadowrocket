# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful.
- Be constructive.
- Stay on-topic.
- Avoid harmful, abusive, or misleading content.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment
- Hate speech
- Personal attacks
- Instructions for bypassing paid features, payment systems, or platform security

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, close issues, or block users who violate this Code of Conduct.
